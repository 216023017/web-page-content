Historic Places in Cape Town

City Hall - 5min walk

1. City Hall was built in 1905.

2. Nelson Mandela made his first public speech here.

3. It has a beautiful clock tower.

Castle of Good Hope - 8min walk

1. Castle of Good Hope is the oldest colonial building in South Africa.

2. Built between 1666 and 1679 by the Dutch East India Company.

3. It served as a maritime replenishment station.

District Six Museum - 2min walk

1. Museum dedicated to preserving the history of District Six.

2. Represents the forced removals during apartheid.

3. Features personal stories and artifacts.
